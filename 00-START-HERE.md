## Quick Setup

### Step 1 - Download

Download the Zouk package as a zip file from your account.

### Step 2 - Add to Your Workspace

Open your project folder. Unzip or move the Zouk folder into your workspace.

### Step 3 - Ask Your AI Coding Tool to Set It Up

Open Claude Code, Codex, or your preferred AI coding environment.

Use plain language like:

Please set up the Zouk skills, agents, loadouts, and workflows for this workspace.

### Step 4 - Confirm the Files Are Available

Check that the skills, agents, loadouts, workflows, docs, and resources are visible in your workspace.

### Step 5 - Start Small
