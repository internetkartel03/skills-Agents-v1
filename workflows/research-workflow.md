# Workflow: Deep Research Sprint
> Category: Research

## Purpose
Conduct structured deep research on any topic — market, technical, scientific, or competitive.

## Skills to Load
- `deep-research`
- `iterative-retrieval`
- `search-first`
- `exa-search`
- `market-research` (for business/competitive topics)
- `scientific-thinking-literature-review` (for academic topics)

## Steps
1. Define the research question clearly in one sentence
2. Activate `search-first` — identify best sources and search strategy
3. Activate `deep-research` — execute multi-pass research
4. Activate `iterative-retrieval` — extract and synthesize findings
5. Produce a structured output (brief, report, or memo)

## Output Formats
- Research brief (1–2 pages)
- Source-annotated findings
- Competitive landscape summary
- Technical feasibility summary

## Time Estimate
20–60 minutes depending on topic depth.
